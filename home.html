Welcome to Bunjako Beach

Your perfect getaway for sun, sand, and serenity.

Discover Paradise

Bunjako Beach offers pristine sands, crystal-clear waters, and a tranquil atmosphere perfect for relaxation and adventure. Located on the scenic shores of Lake Victoria, it's an idyllic spot for families, couples, and solo travelers alike.

Escape the hustle and bustle and immerse yourself in the natural beauty of Uganda. Whether you're looking to unwind by the water, engage in exciting activities, or simply enjoy a breathtaking sunset, Bunjako Beach has something for everyone.

We pride ourselves on providing a memorable experience with a touch of local charm.

Why Choose Bunjako?

Stunning Location

Beautiful views and direct access to Lake Victoria's shores.

Family Friendly

Safe and enjoyable for all ages, with activities for kids.

Exciting Activities

From boat rides to beach volleyball, there's always something to do.

Fun Under the Sun

Boat Rides

Explore the serene waters of Lake Victoria with our guided boat tours. Try your hand at fishing and catch the local tilapia!

Beach Volleyball & Games

Join a friendly game of beach volleyball or enjoy other beach games. Fun for all skill levels and ages.

Gardens & Relaxation

Soak up the sun on our comfortable loungers, or find a shady spot under a palm tree to read a book and unwind.

Bar & Meat

Bunjako is home for drinks and good times. Bring your crew and have a taste of our muchomo with a cold drink in your hand.

Picnics in the sun

Enjoy a picnic on the shores of lake victoria in the sun with tha refreshing lake breeze.

Sunset Vibes

End your day with a spectacular Bunjako sunset, often accompanied with unmatched thrills and chills.
